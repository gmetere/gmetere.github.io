Practical Info

Location

The workshop will take place at the Department of Mathematics of the Università degli Studi di Milano, Via Saldini 50, Milano, Italy. To get information on how to reach the Department, please go to our direction's page.

Accommodation

Maybe, the best thing you can do is to look for your best choice on the web, (the Department is located in Città Studi district). We suggest to book soon, in order to get best rates. If this can help, here you can find a list of hotels nearby the Department.

Registration

If you wish to attend the workshop, please contact us. There is no registration fee.
