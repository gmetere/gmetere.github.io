Selected posts

Professor Aurelio Carboni passed away the 11th December 2012. It has been a great loss to the Category Theory community, and for the Mathematical community in general.

Here we gathered some selected posts from the Category Theory Distribution List.

Conference Home

This is the website of the meeting in memory of Aurelio Carboni. We would like to remember Aurelio's mathematical vision through the voices of his closest friends and colleagues.

The Conference will be held in Milan, June 24 - 26, 2013, at the Department of Mathematics "Federigo Enriques" of the Università degli Studi.

The following invited speakers have already confirmed their participation

  • Dominique Bourn (Université du Littoral, Calais, F)
  • Marta Bunge (McGill University, Ca)
  • Marino Gran (Université catholique de Louvain, Be)
  • Marco Grandis (Università degli Studi di Genova, I)
  • George Janelidze (University of Cape Town, ZA)
  • Peter T. Johnstone (University of Cambridge, UK)
  • F. William Lawvere (University at Buffalo, US)
  • Giuseppe Rosolini (Università degli Studi di Genova, I)
  • Enrico M. Vitale (Université catholique de Louvain, Be)
  • Robert F. C. Walters (Università degli Studi dell'Insubria, I)
  • Richard Wood (Dalhousie University, Ca)

There is no conference fee, but registration is highly appreciated. Everybody is very welcome.
