As in the previuos editions, we would like to have a proper "workshop", with only a few invited talks, some open problem sessions, and some time for informal discussions.

The main speakers will be George Janelidze, Manuela Sobral and Enrico Vitale. They will give an invited 2-hour talk. Besides the three main speakers, there will be invited 1-hour talks by: A. Cigoli, M.M. Clementino, M. Duckerts-Antoine, A. Facchini, J. Gray, Z. Janelidze, L. Marki, B. Noohi, G. Peschke. Finally, Walter Tholen kindly accepted to close the workshop with the last talk.
Although there will be no other scheduled talks, there will be a poster session in order to present and discuss investigations and results.

Program outline

The workshop will start with a welcome buffet on Sunday April 26th at 7.00pm. Talks are scheduled from Monday April 27th, in the morning, till Thursday April 30th, before noon.
