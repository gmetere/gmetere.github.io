Venue and accommodation

CatAlg2018 will take place in Palazzo Feltrinelli, a historical building now belonging to the University of Milan. Besides the conference sessions, lunches will be served here in the Restaurant of the palace.

Gargnano is a touristic little town, so that one can find a wide range of hotels. Participants should make their reservations directly with the hotel. Here follows a list of possible options.
