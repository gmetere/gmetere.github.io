Registration

The registration fee for CatAlg2011 is 120 EURO, it includes:
the welcome buffet, on Tuesday evening, all coffee breaks, conference lunches from Wednesday to Friday.
The registration must be paid cash at the arrival.

In order to register, please fill the registration form and send it by E-mail to the conference address before August 31st:

catalg2011@unimi.it

You will receive a confirmation email as soon as possible.
