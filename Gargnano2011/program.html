Program outline

The idea behind CatAlg2011 is that of a "working" conference, with only a few invited talks, some open problem sessions, and some time for informal discussions.

The main speakers will be Dominique Bourn, George Janelidze and Marco Grandis. They will give an invited 2-hour talk and chair a 1 hour "open problem session", jointly with a member of the Scientific Committee. Besides the three Main Speakers, there will be invited 1-hour talk by: T. Everaert, T. Janelidze, Z. Janelidze, S. Lack, G. Metere, D. Rodelo and T. Van der Linden. Finally, Walter Tholen kindly accepted to close the workshop with the last talk.
Although there will be no other scheduled talks, there will be a poster session in order to present and discuss investigations and results.

The workshop will start with a welcome buffet on Tuesday September 27th, in the late afternoon. Talks are scheduled from wednesday September 28th, in the morning, till Saturday October 1st, before noon.
