Venue and accommodation

The CatAlg2011 will take place in Palazzo Feltrinelli, a historical building now belonging to the University of Milan. Besides the conference sessions, lunch will be served here in the Restaurant of the palace.

Gargnano is a touristic little town, so that one can find a wide range of hotels. Participants should make their reservations directly with the hotel. Agreements have been arranged with some of the hotels listed below, which are close to the conference venue. Refer to CatAlg2011 workshop when making the reservation.
